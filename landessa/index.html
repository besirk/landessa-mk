Taste the Feeling

Landessa Ice Coffee is being prepared based on Arabica coffee beans. Arabica is a synonym of best quality coffee – delicate and charming in its chocolate taste. Landessa – mix of Arabica and fresh, creamy milk, unforgettable taste. Landessa Ice Coffee perfectly refreshes and energizes using its natural coffee power.

Ennstal Milch – the manufacturer of Landessa it is an organization established by farmers who run its cattle breeding on a traditional manner with no artificial fodder no GMO. Landessa Ice Coffee is in one of the most enviroment – friendly packages. Raw materials for the productions comes mainly from renewable sources.

Our Products

Latte Macchiato

So creamy, so light. The best skimmed milk meets aromatic Arabica beans - together they ensure sensual drinking pleasure in between. The fresh alpine milk comes from happy cows in the region and combines valuable ingredients and a rich taste - and that with the lowest fat content. Full enjoyment with no regrets.

Decaf

Voller Kaffeegeschmack ohne Koffein – hier spielt der Geschmack die erste Geige. Für alle, die Kaffeegeschmack lieben und keinen Muntermacher brauchen, ist Landessa Decaf die erste Wahl. Zu jeder Tages- und Nachtzeit vollendeten Kaffeegenuss und cremig-frische Alpenmilch erleben – ganz ohne Koffein. Weil nur der Genuss zählt.

Cappucino

A sensual taste experience: the best fresh alpine milk and fragrant coffee refined with a pinch of cocoa. A recipe for true gourmets. The Arabica beans develop their incomparable aroma in the fresh skimmed milk and form an irresistible symbiosis with the cocoa. Every sip a break. Our cappuccino is available in a practical mug and in the environmentally friendly CartoCan.

Espresso

Holt das Beste aus der Arabica Bohne heraus – Landessa Espresso ist ein erfrischender Muntermacher, bei dem der Genuss nicht zu kurz kommt. Ein Schuss Magermilch und Rahm runden das kräftige Kaffee-Aroma perfekt ab. Ein vollmundiger Wake-Up-Call für alle Sinne.

Caffe Latte

Fresh whole milk and the finest notes of Arabica beans - a poem about an iced coffee for those who love it creamy and full-bodied. The Landessa Caffe Latte is not stingy with its charms and trumps itself in terms of creaminess and elegance. The finest coffee and the best fresh alpine milk in harmony for complete enjoyment.

Vanilla

Aromatic arabica beans, creamy milk and a hint of vanilla - people go into raptures at Landessa Vanilla. An iced coffee like a dessert that flatters not only the palate but also the soul and comes across as carefree. No wonder that vanilla is one of the most popular varieties and is therefore available in cups and CartoCan!
